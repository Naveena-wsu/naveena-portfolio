About Me

Hi, I’m Naveena Punugupati. I work in Site Reliability Engineering and DevOps, with hands-on experience across AWS, Azure, Kubernetes, Terraform, CI/CD pipelines, and monitoring tools.


My work is mainly focused on keeping systems stable, easier to monitor, and smoother to deploy. I enjoy working through production issues, understanding what actually went wrong, and improving systems so the same problems do not keep happening again.


I have worked in environments where things were not always perfect — noisy alerts, deployment failures, unclear logs, and production issues under pressure. Those situations helped me grow as an engineer because they pushed me to look deeper into logs, metrics, infrastructure, and system behavior instead of just applying temporary fixes.


I’m also interested in distributed systems, reliability, and cloud infrastructure, and I’m planning to pursue a PhD in this space to deepen my understanding of large-scale systems.

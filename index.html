About Me

I am a Site Reliability Engineer and DevOps professional with hands-on experience in cloud infrastructure, Kubernetes deployments, CI/CD pipelines, monitoring, and production support.


My work has mostly been around making systems more stable and easier to operate. I have worked through real production challenges like noisy alerts, deployment failures, unclear logs, and environment issues. Those situations helped me become more patient, more detail-oriented, and more focused on root cause analysis instead of quick temporary fixes.


I enjoy working with teams, understanding system behavior, improving automation, and learning how large-scale systems can be designed to fail less and recover faster.
